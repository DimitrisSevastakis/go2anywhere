fuzzy_bookmarks

This extension for chrome allows you to search through your bookmarks in a similar way to Sublime Text

By default, it searches through your bookmarks looking for a "fuzzy" match on the title of the bookmark and exact match on the url of the bookmark.

Searching with the '@' symbol before your query it searches only for exact match on the title. Searching with the '#' symbol before your query it searches only for exact match on the url.
